About

With over 12 years of experience across development and humanitarian contexts, I specialize in designing and implementing research and MEAL systems that produce actionable, evidence-based insights for programs serving the most vulnerable.

Services

How I Can Help Your Organization

Whether you need a robust MEAL framework, a rigorous impact evaluation, or strategic capacity building, I deliver evidence you can act on.

◈

MEAL System Design

End-to-end Monitoring, Evaluation, Accountability & Learning systems that drive program quality and evidence-based decision-making across complex humanitarian and development settings.

◇

Research & Impact Evaluation

Rigorous research design including Randomized Control Trials, quasi-experimental studies, and mixed-methods evaluations with peer-reviewed publication quality.

△

Data Science & Analytics

Advanced quantitative and qualitative analysis using STATA, SPSS, R, and Atlas.ti — from descriptive statistics to inferential modeling and predictive analytics.

○

Capacity Building & Training

International training on assessment tools (IDELA), research methods, digital data collection, and MEAL best practices for organizations across Africa and the Middle East.

□

Program Strategy & Design

Log-frame development, theory of change, proposal writing, and strategic program design informed by evidence — with a track record of securing competitive grants.

▽

Accountability Systems

Design and implementation of Complaints & Feedback Mechanisms, program quality benchmarks, and transparent stakeholder engagement frameworks.
